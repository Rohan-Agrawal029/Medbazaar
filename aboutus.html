About Us


In today's world, science and technology has been accomplishing fascinating advancements and has been catapulting human life standards to even greater level. Sooner than later, the whole world will be beamed with such innovations.

This project is another innovation, which revolutionizes the way we look at the medicinal market, especially online. The customer goes to the shop and purchases the medicine required. A lot of time is wasted and the person gets tired. If he wants to exchange the product, once again he goes to the shop and replaces them. The complete process depends on the physical interactions. The Meta Search Engine collects data from various online pharmaceutical websites regarding the medicine specified by the customer and displays the data in a collective and organized manner. The data primarily consists of the medicine's image, brand, cost, quantity and other required specifications.

The website will be easy to use and order. The customer if needed can create a login account and fill in the required details like their Name, Phone No, Email ID etc in order to keep track of their searches. The customer is able to view the status of the medicine and selects the required medicine, they are then redirected to the selected website by a single click. The business goal for this application is to provide the medicines to all the customers and Admin will provide the database for Meta Search of other online websites.
